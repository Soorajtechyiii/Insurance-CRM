Questions

How does this system work?

▼
The system will make an agreement between you (the policyholder) and an insurance company with your permission only. You pay a regular fee called a premium, and in return, the company promises to cover specific financial risks — like accidents, illness, damage, or loss.

How much is the risk?

▼
In insurance 'risk' means “What is the chance something bad will happen, and how much will it cost?”.So in insurance is all upon taking fundamental rights for you and your family to keep your family safe

Do I need special skills?

▼
You don’t need any special skills to use or buy insurance. Most insurance processes are simple and designed for everyday people. You can easily purchase a policy online or through an agent, pay your premiums, and file a claim if needed — all without technical knowledge. Basic reading skills and access to a smartphone or bank account can make things easier.

How do I get started?

▼
Getting started with insurance is simple and straightforward. First, decide what type of insurance you need — it could be health, life, car, travel, or home insurance depending on your situation. Once you've decided, research a few trusted insurance companies online or talk to a licensed agent. Compare plans based on coverage, premium, claim process, and customer reviews. After choosing the best one, you can apply online or offline by providing some basic details like your name, age, ID proof, and sometimes a health or vehicle check.
