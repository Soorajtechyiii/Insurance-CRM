VEHICLE INSURANCE

Vehicle insurance is a type of financial protection that covers damages or losses involving motor vehicles. It provides coverage for incidents such as accidents, theft, natural disasters, or third-party liability. By paying a regular premium, vehicle owners can reduce the financial impact of repairs, medical expenses, or legal liabilities resulting from unforeseen events. Most countries require at least basic insurance by law to ensure road safety and responsibility. Vehicle insurance not only protects the policyholder but also offers peace of mind while driving.
